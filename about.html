The Story Behind Cirrobound

I spent years working as a senior cloud administrator — sitting inside organizations that dealt with exactly the kinds of problems I now help solve. Scattered documents. Manual intake processes. Teams spending hours on coordination instead of the work they were actually hired to do.

Throughout that time I developed a genuine passion for automation — not as a buzzword, but as a practical tool for giving people back their time. Watching a manual process get replaced by something that just works is one of the most satisfying things in this field.

What frustrated me was that the same level of cloud and automation expertise that large enterprises pay serious money for was out of reach for so many businesses that needed it just as much. Professional service firms, growing companies, and mid-market teams doing real, complex work — but without a dedicated enterprise IT department or a seven-figure technology budget.

That's exactly what Cirrobound is built to fix. The company is new — the expertise isn't. My goal is simple: bring the same caliber of cloud and automation work that enterprise teams take for granted to every business that's ready to work smarter, regardless of size.

No hype. No unnecessary complexity. Just practical improvements that let your team spend more time on what matters.
