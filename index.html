FAQ

Do you replace existing systems?
No. We focus on improving how your current systems, workflows, and teams operate together.

Do we need AI experience?
No. We help you understand where AI fits and what should be improved before adoption.

Is this only for law firms and engineering firms?
No. Those are strong examples, but CirroBound can help workflow-heavy professional service businesses.

What if we've already tried automation tools?
That's actually common. Most tools fail not because the technology is wrong, but because the underlying workflow wasn't ready for it. Cirrobound starts by fixing the process first — so when automation is added, it actually sticks.

Do you work with small firms?
Yes. Many of our engagements are with teams of 2 to 20 people. Smaller firms often have the most to gain — every hour saved has a bigger impact when your team is lean.
